Magic for Beginners

Information for the new MTG Player. Learn about the formats and the decks for each them.

Legacy

Legacy is a constructed format and therefore adheres to the following constructed rules:

  • Minimum of sixty cards
  • No maximum deck size
  • Up to fifteen cards in your sideboard, if used

With the exception of basic lands (Plains, Island, Swamp, Mountain, and Forest), your combined deck and sideboard may not contain more than four of any individual card, counted by its English card title equivalent.

Legacy decks may consist of cards from all Magic card sets, plus the following cards: Sewers of Estark, Windseeker Centaur, and Nalathni Dragon.

Combo

A combo deck is usually a combination of cards that instantly, or very quickly, win the game when combined

Storm

Info

A combo deck that wins through the mechanic storm..

Signature Cards

Strengths

Weaknesses

  • Many deks mainboard hate cards.
Sneak and Show

Info

Sneak Show is a deck that uses both Sneak Attack and Show and Tell to drop a large creature, usually Emrakul, the Aeons Torn and Progenitus into play for the win.

Signature Cards

Strengths

  • Can win on turn one.
  • Has multiple win conditions.
  • Powerful.

Weaknesses

  • Again many decks have mainboard hate.

Aggro

Much like its other counterparts, it's all about winning fast.

Burn

Info

Burn is a deck that uses efficient creatures and Red instant and sorcery spells to deal direct damage to your opponent in an effort to end the game as quickly as possible using little to no disruption

Signature Cards

Strengths

  • Fast
  • Consitent
  • Relatively cheap
  • Easy to learn.

Weaknesses

  • Linear.
  • Combo decks
  • No hate cards.
Merfolk

Info

Merfolk is a blue-based aggro deck with tempo elements that is built around the synergistic elements available to the Merfolk creature type. Merfolk is a mostly fair, highly linear, proactive deck that employs lots of cheap, efficient creatures along with Æther Vial and some catchall disruption in order to overwhelm its opponents.

Signature Cards

Strengths

  • Affordable
  • Constitent
  • Versatile.

Weaknesses

  • Mass removal
  • Spot removal
  • No removal.
Delver

Info

Delver atempts to recreate the power of the burn engine but sacrifices some of the speed for protection and consistency.

Signature Cards

Strengths

  • Constitent
  • versatile.

Weaknesses

  • Burn
  • Unfair decks

Midrange

Midrange decks are slower creature-based decks who trump the speed of fast aggro with better quality from their somewhat more expensive spells.

Stoneblade

Info

Stoneblade is a group of W/x decks that utilize Stoneforge Mystic in order to generate threats through very powerful equipment.

Signature Cards

Strengths

  • Many different variants
  • Usually good against other blue decks.

Weaknesses

  • Midrange decks
Shardless BUG

Info

Shardless BUG is a Sultai-colored midrange deck built around the card Shardless Agent. It's a grindy deck built to feast on "fair" decks such as Delver and other creature-based decks.

Signature Cards

Strengths

  • Built to beat other fair decks.

Weaknesses

  • Weak to combo.

Control

A control deck is a combination of cards that aims to control the opponent's cards and progression with, ideally, the end result where one has full control of everything that is done during the game.

Death and Taxes

Info

A control deck that primarily uses creatures to do the controlling.

Signature Cards

Strengths

  • Good against both fair and unfair decks.

Weaknesses

  • Skill intensive.
  • Very slow.
Lands

Info

A control deck that wins through lands, no creatures and very few non-land spells.

Signature Cards

Strengths

  • Can win on turn 2
  • Most unique deck in legacy.

Weaknesses

  • The price tag is astronomical.
  • Can be very slow.
  • Unfavorable matches are quite common
Blue/White Miracles

Info

Miracle control is a Blue white centric deck that focuses on delaying the game by using sensei's divining top and counterbalance to perform a "soft lock" on your opponent.

Signature Cards

Strengths

  • A lot of favorable matchups.
  • The longer the gmae goes the more likely you are to win.

Weaknesses

  • Slowest deck in legacy.
  • Requires a lot of skill.
